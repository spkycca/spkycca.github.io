Hardware List

5 Different Types of Hardware Within a Computer


Hardware Name Purpose Price Delicacy
Motherboard The motherboard is the main component
in a computer. It is in charge of connecting
almost every part in a computer. Without
a motherboard, it is impossible for a
normal computer to run.		 Low End Motherboard: Starting at ~$100

High End Motherboard: Starting at ~$210		 A motherboard is somewhat delicate, though it can withstand some heat
and force, it is not have substantial stength.
However, motherboards can easily be damaged and destroyed
if bent or comes into contact with water.
Hard Drive A hard drive is the most popular
method of storing permanent data.
Your computer most likely using a HDD
(Hard Drive) in order to store data. This
data includes things such as software
and operating systems.		 Low End HDD: Starting at ~$80

High End HDD: Starting at ~$180		 A hard drive is completely encased. This makes is very durable as long as no liquids or unwanted substances get inside of the component.
PCI Express Video Card The Video/Graphics card is in charge of
displaying the information on your monitor(s).
This part is very important as it allows for easy
user interaction. Video cards are also used for
operations such as bitcoin mining. However,
mining requires a proper video card. Most consumer grade and gaming cards will not work and will overheat easily.		 Low End Graphics Card: Starting at ~$80

High End Graphics Card Starting at $700		 Depending on the card, video cards
can range from delicate to tough. Some
lower end cards are not encased, making them easily damaged by heat,
liquid, other subtances or drops.
Higher end cards are usually completely encased. These encased cards also hgave fans in them, allowing the card to run better without overheating.
Power Supply The power supply allows the computer to run. It supplies electricity to all parts of a computer. This includes parts such as the CPU, the Video Card and the Motherboard. Without a power supply, a computer cannot run. Low End Power Supply: Starting at ~$50

High End Power Supply: Starting at ~$90		 Power supplies, or PSUs, are very
durable parts. As long as nothing unwanted enters the unit, and it is properly cooled, the PSU will last a
fair bit of time. It is a larger part and can be very heavier compared to other parts.
DVD Drive The DVD Drive is a component that allows the computer to read, download, retrieve and send things to a DVD. Although it is not a needed part in a computer, it can be very useful. Low End Starting at ~$25

High End Starting at ~$45		 Finally, DVD Drives are another very
stable and tough part. They are
completely encased, unless the tray is out. Again, if nothing unwanted enters the drive, it should last quite a while as it is a very tough component of a computer.
